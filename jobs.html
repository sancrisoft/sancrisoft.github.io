About Sancrisoft

Sancrisoft is a company of highly qualified professionals with solid experience on Internet based solutions. We work with modern and proven technologies, at competitive rates, focused on quality, but with a wider purpose in mind: The customer goals! We create websites primarily using ReactJS a powerful library to build interactive UIs, also love to build mobile apps for iOS and Android, as well as, custom made information systems.

Learn more about our services and technologies we work with.

Position Description

We're looking for a Full Stack Developer to join our team, we work on multiple projects for clients distributed in different countries. In this position you'll apply your HTML5 / CSS3 / JS skills and learn top modern web technologies such as NodeJS, Express, ReactJS, Redux, Webpack, etc and some of React Native to built Mobile Applications. You will work on Front-End and Back-End tasks to build top-notch products, we look for talented people who want to solve problems for our customers with a good attitude and striving for the quality of their work.

We have a lot of exciting projects in the pipeline, so if you want to be part of one of them keep reading and drop us a line.

Responsabilities

  • Create elegant, efficient and maintainable web applications to support and extend our current projects
  • Troubleshoot customer reported issues
  • Convert designs on HTML / CSS / JS templates
  • Collaborate with other team members and stakeholders

Requirements

  • Proficient understanding of Version Control Systems such as Git
  • Advanced knowledge of HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript
  • Proficient knowledge of cross-browser compatibility issues and ways to work around such issues
  • General understanding of UI/UX design principles and best practices
  • General understanding of Section 508 principles and best practices
  • Basic English and good communication skills
  • Self-taught and highly motivated
  • Proactive and problem solver
  • Passionate in web development, takes ownership and pride in their job
  • Be willing to help your teammates, share your knowledge with them, and learn from them
  • Be open to receiving feedback

Bonus Skills

  • Node and npm
  • Basic understanding of ES6
  • ReactJS and React Native
  • Webpack
  • Redux
  • Foundation / Bootstrap
  • Experience building mobile applications for iOS and Android
  • CMS such as Drupal
