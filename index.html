About Us

Sancrisoft is a company of highly qualified professionals with solid experience on Internet based solutions. We work with modern and proven technologies, at competitive rates, focused on quality, but with a wider purpose in mind: The customer goals! We create websites primarily using ReactJS a powerful library to build interactive UIs, also love to build mobile apps for iOS and Android, as well as, custom made information systems.

Learn more about our services and technologies we work with.

Services

Mobile Apps

We create mobile apps (native and hybrid) for iOS and Android. We also develop web software targeted for mobile devices.

Content Management Systems (CMS)

We work with Drupal, Joomla and Wordpress. We install & configuration CMS, create templates/themes and also build custom components/modules when needed.

Custom Development

Do you need a custom made solution designed for your business only? Sancrisoft can take care of this. We have technical expertise in multiple programming languages and frameworks to build great products.
