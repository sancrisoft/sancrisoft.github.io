About Us

Sancrisoft is a company formed by a group of highly qualified professionals with solid experience in solutions based on Internet. We work with the most modern technologies, with competitive prices, focused on quality, but with a purpose in mind: The client's business goal!. We are experts creating websites based on content management systems such Drupal or Joomla; mobile apps for iOS and Android as well as information systems by developed to fit our client's requirements.

Meet our services and technologies we use.

Services

Mobile Apps

We build native mobile apps for cellphones and tablets running on iOS and Android. We also have expertise in web applications development specifically for mobile devices.

Content Management Systems (CMS)

We have wide experience working with Drupal, Joomla and Wordpress, from installing and configuring the CMS, creating templates or themes, to building custom components / modules to meet our customer's needs.

Custom Developments

Do you need a custom solution specifically for your business? Sancrisoft can take care of it. We have technical expertise in multiple programming languages and development frameworks to build your product.
